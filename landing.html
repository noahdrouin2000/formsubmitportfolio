à propos_

Hello! My name is Noah and I enjoy building websites and all sorts of things on the internet! I'm a web developer from Quebec, Canada. I’ve been doing this for about a year and a half. It all started with trying to build a website for a relative, and since then I’ve fallen in love with this career even more every day! Of course, I’ve done a lot of projects to this day using mostly HTML, CSS, and Javascript. My goal these days is to build practical, yet easy-to-use products and websites for a variety of clients!
